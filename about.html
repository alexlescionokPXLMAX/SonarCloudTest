Hi there, I'm Alex, and I am a composer and producer.

I have been involved in music since I was 7 years old, and have experienced working across a wide-range of music genres, from classical orchestral and indie-pop, to ambient electronic and extreme death metal. I love creating music that combines different influences into one cohesive vision, and have most recently been on a journey of blending noise and found sounds into electronic music.
