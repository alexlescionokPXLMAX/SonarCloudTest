Media Collaborations

All Will Be Well

A gritty short drama.

My masters degree culminated in a final negotiated project, as part of which I created a short drama. In this project I explored the use of found sounds, meaning sounds not typically associated with music, using which I created a soundtrack for the film. All Will Be Well explored the theme of drugs, death, and youth, all of which I wished to explore in the soundtrack.

Twists and Turns – road safety video campaign from The Lemon Twist on Vimeo.

Twists and Turns

Twists and Turns is a road safety video campaign encouraging pedestrians to be more vigilant when crossing the roads. The campaign is targeted at all age groups, with a particular focus on the young adults.

Twists and Turns fused the elegance of ballet and urban complexity of the modern city, both of which served as inspiration for the soundtrack. I focused on piano and traditional orchestral elements and combined these with electronic drums and percussion to reflect the imagery and support the pace of the visuals.

"Very talented and professional, he composed a piece that truly elevated the visuals, constantly showing genuine interest in exploring the creative process of videomaking and being always open to suggestions. I highly recommend Alex as a music composer and I look forward to working together again." - Rino Pucci, director of Twists and Turns

Nursery Crimes

A mixed-media dark comedy and horror play.

Nursery Crimes had a satirical tone, and used nursery rhymes as the primary stimulus. I created a soundtrack for both live and pre-recorded scenes of the play, with the aim of emphasizing fear and comedy. I also added further layers the nursery rhymes in order to accentuate the tension they created.

"Working with Alex was a great pleasure, he was highly communicative and had a really clear vision for how he saw the project’s music. Alex was also very good at taking on our feedback and altering the music accordingly; aligning it more in tone and style with the piece." - James Ormrod, director of Nursery Crimes

MS: Life Changer

Radio documentary about MS and the effects of the illness.

Dealing with real stories, and filled with emotional content, the brief asked for the music to be uplifting in order to promote hope. I composed four tracks, each of which suported a different section of the documentary. I also partially edited the voice audio of the project.

"I found Alex really helpful throughout my whole experience & would definitely recommend!" - Katrina Halsall, creator of MS: Life Changer

King Of The Mountain

The director's cut of the winter JD#Now 19/20 campaign.

The brief asked for the music to fill in the gaps between the monolue, work alongside the existing sound design, and provide a dramatic impact during key moments. I decided to create an ambient soundscape, which expressed high altitude and vast space, which grew larger as the video progressed.

Blue Boy

A modern-day drama following a young closeted protagonist on his journey of realisation and self-discovery, all whilst trying to find his online lover.

I created an indie-inspired soundtrack that highlighted youth and adventure, and supported the short film in shedding a creative light on the experiences of young gay adults. Since its release in 2019, Blue Boy has reached over 340,000 views on YouTube.

"Working with Alex was one of the greatest collaborative relationships I’ve had, he knew exactly the kind of sound I wanted and managed to achieve something that sounded so incredibly perfect for the project that I still can’t believe he made it! Highly recommend." - Ben Massey, director of Blue Boy
